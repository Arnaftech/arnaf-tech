Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At ArNaF Tech, we value your trust. This policy describes how we handle information in our mobile applications.

1. Data Collection: Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information. Features like Prayer Times and Qibla require location data, which is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services: We may use Google Play Services for app updates. These services may collect information used to identify you in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

3. Security: We value your trust in providing us your location for prayer times, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.

4. Contact Us: If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@arnaftech.com.
